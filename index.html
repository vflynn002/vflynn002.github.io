Harry Potter Saves His Friends

Help Harry find his friends.

Voldemort has captured Ron and Hermione. You (Harry Potter) must defeat the villians and save your friends before time is up.

  2. Beware of the dark lord. If you encounter him before you see Ron and Hermione, you have been defeated.
  3. If you find Ron and Hermione, you have succeeded.
